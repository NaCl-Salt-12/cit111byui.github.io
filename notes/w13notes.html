Week 13 Outcomes

  • Final Assesments

Before the Week Begins

  • Students will go over the study guide and come to the first class of the week with any questions they might have.
  • Consider reviewing the teaching tips and suggestions in the CIT111 - W13 Course Group discussion https://byui.instructure.com/courses/70298/discussion_topics/1457734.

During the Week

  • First of the week:
    • Review for the exams.
  • Mid-week
    • There will be two days with two finals. You can have them take them both the last week of regular class or have them just take the coding final the last week of regular class and leave more time for reviews if you choose and then they would take the multiple choice final during testing week.

    • Grading/Feedback

    • Instructor: The Coding Final is partially auto-graded so they get some of the points right away. Remind them that the rest of the points come when the instructor or TA grades each submitted query. The multiple choice final is auto-graded.

    Looking Ahead

    • Remind students that they need to check canvas and all their scores before you submit grades.
