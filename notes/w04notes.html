Note: The weekly Teaching Notes only identify week-specific considerations. Please refer to the General Teaching Notes for repeating course facilitation expectations.

Week Outcomes

  • Learn about what a relational database modeling system is
  • Understand what a relational database is

Before the Week Begins

  • Students will go over preparation materials and complete the preparation check before class on Monday.
  • Consider reviewing the teaching tips and suggestions in the CIT111 - W01 Course Group discussion https://byui.instructure.com/courses/70298/discussion_topics/1457734.

During the Week

  • First of the week:
    • The students should have completed the preparation materials and check before the beginning of the week and be ready for learning, activities, and discussions.

            (W04 - Relational Databases)

    • Take the ERD you showed last week with their gathered data and now show them the tables. Explain again the tables and relationships involved. Explain that this is what makes a relational database relational. Talk about primary and foreign keys that were used. Show the design view of a table and also go over Not Null, Default values and Auto-increment and what they mean. Look at the data in each table and understand how primary keys won't repeat, but foreign keys might with a many relationship.
    • Remind students of the quiz on Wednesday, each Wednesday there will be a quiz. 
  • Mid-week
    • The quiz this week will only be on datatypes.
    • Have them complete their quiz by themselves once and then with others for the second attempt. The discussions and debates during the second attempt as a group is a great 'teach one another' activity. The two scores will be averaged for their final quiz score.

(W04 - SQL Intro):

    • Show them basic SQL queries with the data and then the more advanced queries that were time-consuming and difficult with spreadsheets and how much more efficient they are now with the database. Go over basic SQL commands as a bit of a preview but not teaching them yet. 
  • End of the Week
    • The homework due Saturday will be
      • following along with the MySQL Workbench videos and completing any instructions given in the (W04 - Exploring Workbench). Point out to the students that following the 4 videos there are instructions to follow and there will be two screenshot to take in those instructions.

Grading/Feedback

  • Instructor: The check and quiz are auto-graded. The homework will checking their screenshots. One screen shot is showing the two new patterns in 'PK Column Name: %table%_id and 'Column Name:' %column%. The second screen shot should show their schema list (they should not have a store schema and the mysql schema should be hidden.

Looking Ahead

  • Remind students that they have readings and videos to watch and a check to complete before Monday to be prepared for the next week.
